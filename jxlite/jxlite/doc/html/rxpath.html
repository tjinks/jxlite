XPath Support in JxLite

JxLite @Path annotations support a (very) restricted subset of XPath. Specifically:
  • Only relative paths are supported. (I.e. a path cannot start with / or //).
  • The . and .. operators are not supported.
  • Wildcards are not supported.
  • Predicates are not supported.

Here are some examples of valid @Path annotations:

@Path("controls") @Path("controls/button") @Path("controls/button/@id") @Path("a:controls/a:button/@id")
